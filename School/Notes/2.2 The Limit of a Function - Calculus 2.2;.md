If any of the following conditions hold, the the line x = a is a **vertical asymptote** of f(x):

$$ \lim_{x → a\color{#FF6666}^{-}}  f(x)= +∞ \text{ or} -∞
$$

$$ \lim_{x → a^{\color{#FF6666}+}}  f(x)= +∞ \text{ or} -∞
$$

$$ \lim_{x → a}  f(x)= +∞ \text{ or} -∞
$$
